Frequently asked questions

Reverse logistics, clearly explained

Find answers to common questions about electronic scrap collection, shipment, verification, and payment. Our process is transparent, secure, and designed for all contributors.

Who can use this service?

Individuals, companies, and collection partners can all participate. Whether you have a single device or large quantities of e-waste, our platform is open to everyone looking to dispose of electronics responsibly.

How is the value estimated?

Estimated values are based on a reference table available on the platform. Final pricing is determined after inspection, considering weight, quality, and type of material received.

What happens after I ship items?

Once you ship your electronics, you can track the shipment through your account. After arrival, our team inspects the materials and updates you on the verification process.

How do I receive payment?

After your items are verified, payment is processed instantly via PIX. You will receive a detailed report and confirmation once the transaction is complete.

What if there are discrepancies?

If the received materials differ from your description, the value is adjusted accordingly. You will be notified of any changes and provided with a transparent breakdown.

Is the process environmentally friendly?

Yes. Our system promotes responsible disposal and supports the circular economy by ensuring all electronic waste is processed in compliance with environmental standards.
